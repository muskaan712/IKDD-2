Mission

IKDD is the professional chapter of SIGKDD in India which was established as a part of ACM India in 2011. Our mission is to build and strengthen interest and expertise across academic and industrial institutions in India in the theory and applications of artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, databases, data mining and their related areas.

Activities We Do
Bringing People Together

Towards this mission, IKDD aims to bring together students and experts, academics and practitioners, recruiters and candidates through the organization of conferences, workshops, lecture series, and seminars. These help to showcase latest scientific advancements, encourage collaboration and generate interest in the sciences of data management and analysis.
